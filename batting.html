Top runs scorer in PSL 2016

In the 2016 PSL edition, Umar Akmal was the top scorer while playing for the Lahore Qalandars team. He scored the most runs in PSL 2016 season. He played 7 matches and scored 335 runs with an average of 83.75. His strike rate of 157 shows that he was in a prime form in that season.

Top runs scorer in PSL 2017

The second season of the Pakistan Super League was also dominated by the Akmal brothers but this time Kamran Akmal kept the top spot of the batters list with the most runs in 2017 PSL. Kamran Akmal played a vital role to win his team Peshawar Zalmi the first PSL title. He played 11 matches for Peshawar Zalmi in the 2017 season and scored 353 runs including a blistering knock of 104 runs in the 3rd qualifying final vs Karachi Kings.

  • Runs Scored: 353
  • Average:32.09

Top runs scorer in PSL 2018

In 2018, Islamabad United won the title for the second time in the history of the Pakistan Super League. The addition of third-team Multan Sultan extended the tournament’s period. The leading runs scorer of 2018 PSL was Luke Ronchi (Islamabad United wicketkeeper). Ronchi scored a record 435 runs at the end of the season and played a vital role to clinch the title for his team.
