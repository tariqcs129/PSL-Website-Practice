BOWLING STATISTICS OF PSl

Bowling in T20 cricket can be challenging, and the batsman is always looking to hit the bowlers off their length. Not only that, but the sheer willingness to smash every ball of the match makes it challenging. However, a bowler takes wickets and puts their name in the list of most wickets in one season. When we see in PSL, many great bowlers from all around the globe are part of the competition. Not only that, their sheer will to take a wicket and help the team in winning a competition is something that makes the game in the PSL interesting. Today we will look into the bowlers who have taken the most wickets in one season of PSL. The list maintained here is in serial order.
